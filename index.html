About me

Hello, this is Huipu. I come from Luoyang that is located in North of China. I have been living in Corvallis for 3 years. During this period, I earned my Master's degree in Computer Science (also Minor in Business & Entrepreneurship) from Oregon State University in 2017.

I have always been an achiever not only in my professional area but also in my other life fields. As a self-motivated learner, I'd love to learn new things in my spare time by myself. I can work alone efficiently and also work well on a team. Currently, I'm learning web development and adding some cool things on my personal website by using HTML5, CSS3 & JavaScript.

Except for professional work, my life was full filled with sports, traveling and collections. I go in for hiking, snowboarding & photographing. Moreover, I am a Marvel fan, in particular, Iron-man collector.

My motto in life is to,

If you want something, work hard and you will achieve it; there are no shortcuts.
